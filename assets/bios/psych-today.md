## Jayne Reel

Clinical Social Work/Therapist, LMSW, PMH-C

I help kids and adults struggling with anxiety, OCD, fertility challenges and loss, and perinatal mood and anxiety disorders. I provide an affirming space for LGBTQIA+ clients and families to explore identity and navigate related challenges. If you’re trying to conceive, facing infertility, pregnant, or postpartum, it’s important to find a therapist who “gets it” and specializes in these areas. With advanced training in Perinatal Grief and Loss, Perinatal Mood and Anxiety Disorders and experience working in the NICU, I am uniquely positioned to help you heal from loss or trauma, adjust to parenthood, or navigate building a family.

I use an individualized, collaborative approach to identify your strengths and provide you with the tools to become the best version of yourself. To alleviate symptoms and create lasting change, I use goal-oriented and skill-focused evidence-based treatments, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP).
Reach out to learn more about my background and approach! Our practice, Helm Behavioral Wellness, provides free, 15 minute phone consultations so that you can learn about me before your first appointment. I offer appointments in Brooklyn Heights and online.
