Therapy Services

Learn more about the types of support that Jayne provides.

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy is a private, consistent space to understand what is happening, reduce distress, and build tools that fit your actual life.

Jayne's approach is warm, collaborative, and goal-directed. Sessions may include insight, emotional processing, skills practice, parent support, or exposure-based work depending on your needs.

You do not need to know exactly where to begin. That can be part of the work.

Individual therapy may be a fit if you:

  • Feel anxious, stuck, or overwhelmed
  • Are navigating OCD symptoms or intrusive thoughts
  • Need support during pregnancy, postpartum, or fertility treatment
  • Are processing grief, reproductive loss, or birth trauma
  • Want support for your child or teen
  • Need LGBTQIA+ affirming care
  • Feel burned out from holding too much
  • Want practical tools and deeper self-understanding

What Sessions Look Like

Sessions are collaborative and responsive to what you need that day. The work can be structured and skills-based, reflective and exploratory, or a mix of both.

Getting oriented

You and Jayne clarify what is bringing you in, what has helped before, and what you want therapy to support.

Building tools

Sessions may include coping strategies, CBT skills, exposure planning, grounding tools, communication support, or parent-based guidance.

Practicing change

Jayne helps you apply what you are learning between sessions so progress can show up in everyday routines, relationships, and decisions.

Areas of Focus & Treatment

Anxiety

Support for worry, panic, avoidance, perfectionism, stress, and the physical symptoms of anxiety.

OCD

Evidence-based treatment for obsessions, compulsions, intrusive thoughts, and reassurance cycles using ERP.

Perinatal Mental Health

Care during pregnancy, postpartum, and early parenthood, including PMADs and identity shifts.

Reproductive Loss & Fertility

Support for reproductive grief, fertility challenges, birth trauma, and the complexity of building a family.

Kids, Teens & Parents

Therapy that is engaging for young clients and practical for families navigating school, stress, and change.

LGBTQIA+ Affirming Care

A supportive space for identity, relationships, family dynamics, stress, healing, and self-expression.
