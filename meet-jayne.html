About Jayne Reel, LMSW, PMH-C

A warm, encouraging therapist specializing in anxiety, OCD, and perinatal mental health.

Jayne Reel, LMSW, PMH-C, works with kids, teens, and adults who are navigating anxiety, OCD, perinatal mental health concerns, grief, fertility challenges, and major life transitions.

Her style is steady, practical, and relational. She helps clients build insight, reduce distress, and develop coping tools that can carry into daily life.

Jayne's Approach to Therapy

Jayne's approach is warm, collaborative, and practical. She helps clients understand what is happening beneath the surface while also building tools they can use outside of sessions.

Therapy may be structured and skills-based, more reflective and exploratory, or a mix of both depending on your goals.

Evidence-based

Jayne draws from CBT, ERP, perinatal psychotherapy, and body-based approaches for anxiety, OCD, perinatal mental health, and mood concerns.

Goal-directed

The work stays connected to what you want to change, while leaving room for complexity, emotion, and the pace of real life.

Affirming

As a queer therapist, Jayne offers identity-affirming care for LGBTQIA+ clients and families.

Specialized Training

Jayne has focused training in evidence-based and perinatal mental health treatments.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps clients understand the connection between thoughts, feelings, body sensations, and behavior. Jayne uses CBT to support clients with anxiety, OCD, mood concerns, stress, and patterns that feel hard to interrupt.

Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP)

ERP is the gold-standard treatment for OCD. It helps clients gradually face feared thoughts, sensations, or situations while practicing new responses that reduce compulsions and reassurance cycles over time.

Perinatal Mental Health

Jayne is certified in Perinatal Mental Health through Postpartum Support International and has advanced training in perinatal psychotherapy. She supports clients during pregnancy, postpartum, and the transition into parenthood.

Perinatal Mood & Anxiety Disorders

Jayne has training through The Seleni Institute in perinatal mood and anxiety disorders, including the emotional, behavioral, and body-based experiences that can arise during pregnancy and postpartum.

Perinatal Grief, Loss & Fertility

Jayne has training in perinatal grief and loss through The Seleni Institute and supports clients navigating reproductive loss, fertility challenges, birth trauma, and the complexity of building a family.

Professional Background & Education

Jayne's background includes work in schools, hospitals, bereavement support, and outpatient psychotherapy settings. These experiences give her a grounded understanding of the real-world stressors children, adults, and families face.

Her prior clinical settings include University of Virginia Children's Hospital, including Pediatric and Neonatal ICUs, Bleuler Psychotherapy Center, Carson's Village Bereavement Support, and Abraham Baldwin Middle School.
