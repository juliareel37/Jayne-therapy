Hi, my name is Jayne. I am a warm, goal-directed therapist specializing in anxiety, OCD, and eating disorders.

I work with kids, teens, adults, and families to build insight, reduce distress, and develop practical coping tools that support lasting change.

My approach is individualized, collaborative, and grounded in evidence-based treatments, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP), the gold-standard treatment for OCD.

I also provide identity-affirming care for LGBTQIA+ clients and families, and support people navigating fertility challenges, reproductive loss, pregnancy, postpartum, birth trauma, and Perinatal Mood and Anxiety Disorders.

Background and training include:

  • Perinatal Mental Health Certification (PMH-C)
  • M.S.W. from Columbia University and B.A. in Psychology from Barnard College
  • Advanced training in perinatal grief, loss, mood, and anxiety disorders
  • Experience in school, hospital, bereavement, and psychotherapy settings
