FAQs

What can I expect during the first session?

Our first session is a chance to get to know one another, talk about what brings you to therapy, and begin identifying the goals you would like to work toward.

Do you offer virtual or in-person appointments?

Placeholder answer: Sessions may be available virtually, in person, or in a hybrid format depending on location, scheduling, and client needs.

Who do you work with?

I work with children, teens, adults, parents, and families seeking support with anxiety, OCD, eating disorders, reproductive mental health, grief, and life transitions.

How long does each therapy session last?

Placeholder answer: Standard sessions are typically 45 to 50 minutes. Longer sessions may be recommended for family work or other specific treatment needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Placeholder answer: Insurance and payment options can be discussed during your initial consultation. Documentation for out-of-network reimbursement may also be available.

How often will we meet?

Most clients begin with weekly sessions, but frequency depends on your goals, availability, and treatment plan. We can revisit the schedule as your needs change.

How do I get started?

Start by booking a consultation. We will briefly discuss what you are looking for, answer initial questions, and decide whether working together feels like a good fit.
